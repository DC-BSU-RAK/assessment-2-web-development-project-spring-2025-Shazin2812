Basic Controls

  • Steer: Tap or Tilt (depending on device)
  • Drift: Swipe left/right
  • Nitro: Tap nitro button for speed boost
  • Stunts: Use ramps and control mid-air flips

Tips:

Upgrade your car, take advantage of shortcuts, and manage nitro efficiently to win!
